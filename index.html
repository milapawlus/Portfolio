About Me


I was born and brought up in Poland, it’s been 11 years since I moved to Sweden. I have chosen Sweden because most of my friends are already been living here and they spoke highly about the country. Moreover, Sweden offers good job opportunities and helps to grow in life. For the past few years, I worked in the hotel and catering industry which had been very interesting, giving me the opportunity to work in a team to develop my social and communication skills. Right now I am in a stage of my life where I’m motivated and eager to learn new things. I’m a positive and open-minded person with good communication skills and fluent in speaking English and moderate Swedish. I have been to a Swedish SFI course to learn Swedish, this course helped me a lot to communicate in Sweden. During my spare time, I like to explore the internet, recently I have started to learn front-end programming languages. Since childhood, I was more interested in art and drawing. I was even taking part in many art competitions. I think this interest drove me to learn front-end programming languages. Whenever I try to learn to code, I visualize it as some kind of art and my target is to make it beautiful. Besides, I am also an animal lover.
